The Natural History Section

Our Natural History section offers a fascinating glimpse into the diverse flora and fauna of the Indian subcontinent. From prehistoric fossils to contemporary wildlife, this collection showcases the natural wonders of our region.

Visitors can explore dioramas depicting various ecosystems, examine rare specimens, and learn about conservation efforts to protect India's biodiversity.
